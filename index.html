Overview

Dr. Michele Cannata is a licensed clinical psychologist with a Psy.D. degree in School/Clinical Child Psychology from Pace University. She has provided mental health services in various settings including inpatient and outpatient hospital settings, residential treatment facilities, the public school system and private practice.

Background

Extensive training in child therapy throughout her internship, post doctoral supervision (in a private practice) and school based experiences with knowledge in play therapy, trauma, learning challenges, parenting and family work.

Focus

Private practice treats young children with play therapy but also services adolescents, teens, and adults.
